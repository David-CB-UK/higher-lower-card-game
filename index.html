Welcome to Higher/Lower! Can you predict whether the next card will be higher or lower?

  • Click Draw Card to reveal the first card.
  • Select Higher or Lower to predict whether the next card will be higher or lower.
  • Each correct guess increases your Current Streak.
  • An incorrect guess resets your Current Streak to zero.
  • Equal cards don't count—simply guess again.
  • Best This Game records your highest streak during the current game.
  • Best Ever records the highest streak achieved since the page was loaded, allowing players to challenge one another.
  • The highest possible streak is 52 consecutive correct guesses.
  • Play until the deck is empty, then reshuffle to start a new game.
  • At the end of every game you'll receive a unique score message based on your best streak; aim high & discover them all!

About Higher/Lower

High Low is a browser-based version of the classic Higher or Lower card game. Players predict whether the next card drawn from a standard deck will be higher or lower than the current card, building the longest winning streak possible.

The game is designed to be simple to learn while providing a fun challenge based on probability and a little luck. Equal cards do not end a streak, allowing players to continue playing until an incorrect prediction is made.
